# Problem Statement
You are given two positive integers 
**a**
 and 
**b**
. Let 
**x**
 be the average of 
**a**
 and 
**b**
. Print 
**x**
 rounded up to the nearest integer.

## Constraints
---
* **a**
 and 
**b**
 are integers.

* 1
≤
**a**
,
**b**
≤
100

## Input
----
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:

**a b**

## Output
---
Print 
x
 rounded up to the nearest integer.

 ### Sample Input 1
----
> **1 3**

### Sample Output  1
----
> **2**

**The average of 
1
 and 
3
 is 
2.0
, and it will be rounded up to the nearest integer, 
2
.**

 ### Sample Input 1
----
> **7 4**

### Sample Output  1
----
> **6**

**The average of 
7
 and 
4
 is 
5.5
, and it will be rounded up to the nearest integer, 
6
.**

 ### Sample Input 1
----
> **5 5**

### Sample Output  1
----
> **5**
