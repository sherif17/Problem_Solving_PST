Compute 
A
×
B
.
## Constraints
---
* 1
≤
A

100
* 1
≤
B

100

-----
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:

A
 
B

## Output
---------
Print the value 
A
×
B
 as an integer.

---
2 5

## Sample Output 1

10
